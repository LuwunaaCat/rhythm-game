Deck-building rhythm game, inspired by pokemon, balatro, deltarune, and gunfire reborn
You (band leader) run around the area recruiting creatures to be band members
you must beat each little guy in a short performance before they can join your team
Difficulty of the performance depends on rarity of ability the guy gives you
Rarities are Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, Mythical
three commons can be combined to create an uncommon, three uncommons to a rare, etc
limited space on your team, but larger space in inventory
When you get into a battle with another band leader you need to perform to attack the opponent (trade 4’s)
Attacks happen in 4 bar sections, and range on a scale of 0-100% power; power is determined by how well you perform (meter starts at 0 and power is added (or subtracted) per attack)
Note pattern is pre determined based on and synced to the song playing and runs in the background while the song is playing, however once you select an attack to perform, the chart opens up for performance upon the following 4 bar section, preceded by a “Ready, Set, Go!/2, 3, 4” count in.
The opponent will attack back, damage is calculated on a random number generator, based on the opponents level
each little guy gives you a boon to your performance deck-builder style (extra damage, more grace for note timing, double or nothing etc)
Talk to NPC’s to, level up (more creatures in your band, increased hp), learn new moves, get tips for performing, etc
Pixelated 2d/top-down platformer style (original loz, deltarune, etc)
sections of the game are separated into different screens, that will be switched upon hitting the edge of the current screen
different character sprites for roaming and battling
